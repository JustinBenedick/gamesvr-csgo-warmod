All files must be executed within the console as such : exec bottest.cfg
Some files would have to executed after the first round has started. Do not run a tourney match after running these scripts.

1v1test.cfg  "2 player test"
Allow for a match where you can have 1 player per team and a max of 8 rounds.

2v2test.cfg
Allow for a match where you can have 2 players per team and a max of 8 rounds.

bottest-8round.cfg
Enables a match of bots, 8 rounds, and disables knife round. may need to run /forcestart

bottest.cfg
Enables a match of bots, full 30 rounds, and disables knife round. may need to run /forcestart
